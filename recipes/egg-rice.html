Egg Fried Rice Recipe

Description

This is one of my favorite dishes and goes well with almost all stews.

This particular recipe serves 4 people

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 cup uncooked instant rice
  • 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
  • ½ onion, finely chopped
  • ½ cup green beans
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. In a saucepan bring water, salt and soy sauce to a boil. Add rice and stir.

    -Remove from heat, cover and let stand 5 minutes.

  2. Heat oil in a medium skillet or wok over medium heat.

    -Saute onions and green beans for 2 to 3 minutes.

    -Pour in egg and fry for 2 minutes, scrambling egg while it cooks.

  3. Stir in the cooked rice, mix well and sprinkle with pepper.
