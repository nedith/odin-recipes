Fried Chicken Recipe

Description

Who doesn't love fried chicken!!! Try out my recipe tonight!

This particular recipe serves 4 people

Ingredients

  • 1 (4 pound) chicken, cut into pieces
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1Â½ cups all-purpose flour for coating
  • 2 quarts vegetable oil for frying

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, heat oil over medium heat.
  2. Salt and pepper chicken pieces to taste, then roll in flour to coat.
  3. Place chicken pieces in skillet and fry on medium heat until one side is golden brown,

    -then turn and brown other side until chicken is no longer pink inside and its juices run clear.

  4. Drain on paper towel and serve!
